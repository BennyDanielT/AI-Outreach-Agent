## About the Organization
BlueGrid is a leading company in the field of vessel-to-grid technology, aiming to reduce reliance on fossil fuels and create a sustainable path to net zero. The company works at the convergence of two net zero megatrends: electric marine transportation and net zero electricity grids.

## About the Job
We are seeking a talented DevOps Developer to join our team at Rimot.io. The successful candidate will work on TypeScript and Python codebases, focusing on CICD, pipeline development, and deployment. The job is full-time and based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, with a start date of June 30, 2024.

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop scalable and efficient DevOps pipelines for our vessel-to-grid technology
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of our technology with existing systems
- Develop and maintain high-quality code in TypeScript and Python
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues in a timely and efficient manner
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to the improvement of our development processes

## Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field
- At least 2 years of experience in DevOps, software development, or a related field
- Strong understanding of DevOps principles and practices
- Proficiency in TypeScript and Python programming languages
- Experience with CICD tools and pipeline development
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
